ABOUT

I was born in a small town among the Italian Alps. I have always lived in a beautiful place that has made me grow and appreciate nature, mountains, animals, tranquility and respect for what surrounds us.

I approached the world of photography by chance, when I was a child I found an old Nikon F, lost in the wood and battered, a 'gift of fate' of who knows from what traveler. Shortly thereafter I bought the first film and from that moment the magic of photography conquered me slowly.

In the years I did the first trips, the first photos with bokeh background !!!, like a miracle for the eyes, the first step of a passion that would never have left me.
Today I travel all kinds, I appreciate the amenities but I also love to move with backpack, sleeping bag and tent. I think that every trip has its own dimension and a right way to be lived and travel is also sometimes knowing how to renounce.

For some years now I love to observe and photograph people above all, those special faces that tell with their expressions, features and wrinkles of unknown and precious stories.
I am struck by the beauty of the gazes and simple gestures that I try to capture with a delicate approach, sometimes shy, respecting people and places that I cross. Behind the photos I like to imagine and suggest untold stories, silent words and that subtle beauty of knowing how to grasp little things that make every new encounter special.

My post production tries to be just as delicate, never invasive, I like that expresses, in a natural and not artifact way, the atmosphere of the moment.
For some time I have been trying to travel with professional photographers, these are specific workshops on themes related to portrait photography and travel reports. Small groups of people, now almost family, linked by the same passion.

The meeting with the Italian photographer and now friend Alessandro Bergamini was 'fatal', his approach has literally changed my way of photographing, pointing out new ways to raise the degree of sensitivity and poetics.

MY INSPIRATION/MOTIVATION

My work is inspired by the passing of time, which changes things, which transforms everything, which also changes us, not only by making ourselves grow and then grow old but by constantly changing the vision we have of the world.

This awareness accompanies my travels and inspires my shots, I think of the many places, the people and the fleeting traditions that are disappearing in a quick and silent way, leaving an unbridgeable emptiness. Traveling and discovering the world with respect allows us to be, without even realizing it, privileged witnesses of a history that will not be repeated.

Time escapes us.

Traveling especially, in general when you live more intensely (and for me the two things coincide) happens that our mind is not able to make us fully enjoy that transient present in which we float.

Photography, at least for me, partly alleviates this sense of elusiveness of experiences that sometimes accompanies me on my return from travels.

I entrust my work and aesthetics photography to the task of telling and giving the observer inspiration and emotion.
